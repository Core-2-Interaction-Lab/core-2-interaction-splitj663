Pork Buns

  • Prep Time - 30 Min
  • Marinate Time - Overnight
  • Cook Time - 20 Min
  • Difficulty - Medium

Ingredients

  • Lean Pork
  • Lemon Grass
  • Fish Sauce
  • Soy Sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • Mayo
  • Chili Crisp
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Kimchi
  • Bean Sprouts

Instructions

Cut pork into thin strips and then place in a closeable container.
Place marinating ingredients in (lemon grass, soy sauce, fish sauce)
Marinate Overnight
Combine mayo and chilicrisp to make your aoli

Place cuts of pork on gridle. Be sure to add water to render out the fat
Constantly turn the pork to avoid charring.
